Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

Eisenhower Matrix ("the App") is a free task management application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App handles, how it is stored, and when (if ever) it leaves your device.

1. Data We Collect

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Task data - titles, descriptions, due dates, quadrant assignments, labels, completion status, and recurring task settings.
  • App preferences - theme selection, notification settings, reminder times, and history retention period.
  • Device identifier - a randomly generated ID stored on your device, used solely to identify your backups if you choose to use the backup feature.

The App does not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. No data is sent to any server by default. The App works entirely offline.

3. Optional Backup Feature

The App includes an optional Backup & Sync feature that is off by default. If you choose to enable it, you manually enter a server URL of your choosing. When you initiate a backup, the following data is sent to that URL:

  • Your task data (titles, descriptions, due dates, labels, and settings)
  • Your device identifier (the randomly generated ID described above)
  • A timestamp of the backup

You control where your data goes. The App does not provide, operate, or have access to any backup server. Data is transmitted over HTTPS when your configured server supports it. You can disable or remove the backup URL at any time in Settings.

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services.

5. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications - to send due-date reminders and daily review alerts. You can deny or revoke this at any time in your device settings.
  • Display over other apps - optional, off by default. If you enable the floating Quick Capture bubble, this lets the App show a small button on top of other apps so you can add a task without switching away. You grant this through your device's system settings and can revoke it at any time.
  • Run a foreground service - used only while the floating Quick Capture bubble is active, to keep it visible and responsive. While active, your device shows an ongoing notification. The service does nothing in the background other than display the bubble, and it stops when you dismiss the bubble.
  • Internet access - used only if you configure the optional backup feature.

The App does not use the microphone, camera, location, contacts, or any other sensitive device sensors.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your data with any third party. Data only leaves your device if you explicitly use the backup feature or the export/share function, both of which are user-initiated actions.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Task data remains on your device until you delete it within the App or uninstall the App.
  • Completed task history retention can be configured in Settings (Forever, 30, 90, or 180 days).
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.
  • If you used the backup feature, data stored on your backup server is under your control - manage or delete it through your own server.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
